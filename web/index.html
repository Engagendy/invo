Relationship Rules

                  
Accounting belongs to the company. Projects are operational and costing dimensions under that company.

                

                

                  
`User -> Companies`: one user can own multiple companies and switch the active book.

                  
`Project -> Company`: each project belongs to one company and carries its job-costing identity.

                  
`Accounting -> Company`: chart of accounts, periods, journals, and balances remain company books, not project books.
